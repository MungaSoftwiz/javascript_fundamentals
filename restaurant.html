Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a celebratory rice and meat dish cherished in the Coastal cities. The biryani consists of fluffy basmati rice layered over tender
& succulent pieces of chicken meat, accompanied with the mesmerizing aromas of spices, herbs & caramelized onions. Get to enjoy the meal for only $5.2

Beef Pilau

Pilau is a rice dish that is cooked in a seasoned broth, with some pieces of beef, chicken and sometimes peas, nuts and raisins,
and whose brown color is obtained from being fried in caramelized onions and a blend of spices. This delicious meal costs $4.95

Coconut Fish

Kenyans love a hot delicacy of fish. For the Coastal people they love theirs filleted and stewed it in coconut milk, garlic and lemon.
This delicacy will cost you only $6.5
